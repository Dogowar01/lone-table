The Lone Table

Play

Choose a move, roll the dice, read what happens. Consult the oracle when you need the world to answer a question.

  1. Set up your character first — go to the Character tab and fill in your name and stats (Edge, Heart, Iron, Shadow, Wits — each 1–3).
  2. Swear a vow — go to the Vows tab and give yourself a goal. This is what drives the story.
  3. Back here to play — when your character faces something risky, choose the move that fits the moment from the dropdown below.
  4. Roll the dice — a d6 + your stat = your action score. Two d10s are the challenge dice. Beat both = strong hit. Beat one = weak hit. Beat neither = miss.
  5. Read the outcome — the move tells you exactly what happens on a hit or miss. Let that shape the next scene.
  6. Ask the oracle — when you're not sure what the world does, type a yes/no question and roll. Or pick a table for a random detail.
  7. Write the story — use the Journal tab to record what's happening in your own words.

Character

Your stats shape every roll. Momentum builds as you succeed and can be burned to upgrade a bad result. Start here before your first session.

Identity
Stats (1–3 each)

Edge = speed & evasion · Heart = resolve & persuasion · Iron = strength & combat · Shadow = stealth & deception · Wits = perception & survival

Condition tracks

Tap a pip to set the value. These go down when you suffer harm or stress. If Health or Spirit hits 0, trigger the appropriate Suffer move.

Momentum

Starts at 2. Rises when you get strong hits, falls when you suffer. Range −6 to +10. When momentum is positive and higher than a challenge die, you can burn it to upgrade your outcome — tap Burn in the result screen.

Assets

Special abilities, companions, or gear that give you advantages. Tap to remove.

Bonds

People and communities you have a sworn connection to. Bonds let you add +1d6 when you make certain relationship moves. Tap to remove.

Vows

A vow is a sworn oath that drives your journey. Every vow has a rank — the harder the vow, the more progress boxes it has and the more work it takes to fill them. Fulfil a vow by rolling your progress track against two challenge dice.

World

Setting packs add new oracle tables, location prompts, NPC flavour, and ready-to-play scenario seeds to your game. Each pack is a self-contained JSON file — import one below to unlock its content in the oracle.

What's in a pack?

Every setting pack contains the same building blocks, flavoured for its setting:

  • Location prompts — named, evocative places to discover
  • NPC tables — who lives here, their role and one defining trait
  • Complication table — things that go wrong in ways specific to this world
  • Scenario seeds — a vow already in motion with a hook and first scene
  • Custom oracles — tables unique to the setting (weather, tide, rumour, etc.)

About

The Lone Table is a solo tabletop RPG companion. No GM, no group, no table needed — just you, the dice, and the oracle.
